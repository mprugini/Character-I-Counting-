About Us

~Our Story~

We are a startup located in Toronto, Canada. Founded in November 2017 by a talented group of developers. We are currently offering best in the world character "I" counting service. With our solution, user is able to submit text and see how many times does letter "I" appear. We are constantly trying to improve and use the latest technologies to provide the best in the world service.
